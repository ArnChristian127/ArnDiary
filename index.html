Compilation of Introduction to Digital Graphics Design.

This website is dedicated to showcasing the final practical exam for the course 'Introduction to Digital Graphic.' It serves as a comprehensive platform that compiles and highlights all the activities, projects, and tasks completed throughout the previous terms. The website provides an in-depth look at the various skills and techniques acquired during the course, offering a detailed overview of the student's progression in mastering digital graphic design tools, principles, and concepts.

Midterm Activities

Designing Logo

Our professor demonstrated how to create a logo from scratch, guiding us through the entire process step by step. This included brainstorming design concepts, choosing appropriate shapes, colors, and fonts, and using design tools or software to bring the idea to life.

Designing Calling Card

Our professor demonstrated the process of creating a calling card design, guiding us through each step with careful detail.

Designing Flyer

Our professor demonstrated the process of creating a flyer design, walking us through each step involved. This included understanding the purpose of the flyer, selecting an appropriate layout, incorporating eye-catching visuals, and using typography effectively to convey the message.

Designing Movie Poster

Our professor gave an engaging demonstration on how to create a visually striking movie poster. They walked us through the entire process, starting with brainstorming design concepts, selecting appropriate color schemes and fonts, and incorporating high-quality images and graphics.
